Hike With Us In Ireland

Discover Ireland's Majestic Landscapes

Embark on a journey through Ireland's breathtaking landscapes with us, where every trail tells a story of ancient history, rich culture, and unspoiled natural beauty. From the rugged cliffs of the Wild Atlantic Way to the serene lakes of Killarney, our guided hikes offer an unparalleled way to experience the Emerald Isle's diverse terrain.

Expert Guides with Local Knowledge

Our team of expert guides are not only experienced hikers but also passionate storytellers. With deep local knowledge, they bring Ireland's history and legends to life, making each hike a fascinating adventure. Learn about the myths of the Giant's Causeway, the history of the Wicklow Mountains, and the unique flora and fauna of the Burren as you explore with us.

Connect with Nature & Find Inner Peace

Hiking in Ireland is more than just a physical activity; it's a chance to connect with nature and find inner peace. The tranquil beauty of Ireland's landscapes offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Breathe in the fresh air, listen to the sounds of nature, and feel rejuvenated as you hike through some of the most serene and picturesque places on earth.

Join a Community of Adventurers

When you hike with us, you become part of a community of like-minded adventurers who share a love for the outdoors and a passion for exploration. Forge new friendships, share unforgettable experiences, and create lasting memories as you embark on this incredible journey together.

Kerry Way

Location: County Kerry

Distance: 214 km (133 miles)

Highlights: Killarney National Park, Muckross House, Torc Waterfall

Dingle Way

Location: County Kerry

Distance: 179 km (111 miles)

Highlights: Slea Head, Blasket Islands, Conor Pass

Beara Way

Location: Counties Cork and Kerry

Distance: 196 km (122 miles)

Highlights: Beara Peninsula, Healy Pass, Dursey Island

Western Way

Location: Counties Galway and Mayo

Distance: 250 km (155 miles)

Highlights: Maumturk Mountains, Lough Inagh, Croagh Patrick
